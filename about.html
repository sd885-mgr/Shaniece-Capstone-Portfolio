About Me

My career is defined by a commitment to public service, organizational excellence, and the advancement of equitable workplace practices. With over a decade of foundational experience at Summit Health, I developed a deep understanding of high-stakes service delivery and the vital importance of systems that support both providers and the community.

Currently, I serve as the Employment Relations Manager at Rutgers University–Newark. In this capacity, I bridge the gap between policy and practice, addressing complex employee relations matters and fostering an environment rooted in fairness and institutional integrity.

Parallel to my professional roles, my academic journey at Rutgers has been a pursuit of excellence. Having graduated Magna Cum Laude, I was honored to be inducted into the Pi Alpha Alpha Honor Society. Now, as an MPA candidate, I am refining my expertise in leadership and policy analysis.

"My passion lies in ethical leadership, inclusive workplace practices, and improving systems that directly impact individuals and communities."
