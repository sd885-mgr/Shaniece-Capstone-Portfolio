About Me

My career is defined by a commitment to public service, organizational excellence, and the advancement of equitable workplace practices. With over a decade of foundational experience at Summit Health, I developed a deep understanding of high-stakes service delivery and the vital importance of systems that support both providers and the community. This tenure in healthcare instilled in me a resilient, person-centered approach to administration that I carry into every professional endeavor.

Currently, I serve as the Employment Relations Manager at Rutgers University–Newark. In this capacity, I bridge the gap between policy and practice, addressing complex employee relations matters and fostering an environment rooted in fairness and institutional integrity. My work is focused on promoting organizational effectiveness through strategic human resources management and advocacy for inclusive practices.

Parallel to my professional roles, my academic journey at Rutgers has been a pursuit of excellence. Having graduated Magna Cum Laude with a Bachelor’s in Public Affairs and Administration, I was honored to be inducted into the Pi Alpha Alpha Honor Society. Now, as a Master of Public Administration (MPA) candidate, I am refining my expertise in leadership and policy analysis. My vision is to leverage this combined experience in healthcare and higher education to lead public organizations that are not only efficient but deeply ethical.

"My passion lies in ethical leadership, inclusive workplace practices, and improving systems that directly impact individuals and communities."
