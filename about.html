About Me

I am a public administration professional with a strong foundation in leadership, human resources, and healthcare service delivery. I currently serve as an Employment Relations Manager at Rutgers University–Newark, where I support organizational effectiveness by addressing complex employee relations matters and promoting equitable workplace practices.

In addition to my work in higher education, I have over a decade of experience in healthcare through my role at Summit Health. This experience has strengthened my ability to understand the real-world impact of public service delivery, particularly in high-demand and diverse environments.

"My passion lies in ethical leadership, inclusive workplace practices, and improving systems that directly impact individuals and communities."
