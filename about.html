About Me

Shaniece L. Dillahunt, MPA Candidate

✨ Public Administration Professional | Employment Relations Manager | Healthcare Advocate

I am a public administration professional and Master of Public Administration candidate at Rutgers University–Newark, with an anticipated completion in May 2026, and a strong foundation in leadership, human resources, and healthcare service delivery. My work is centered on applying policy and organizational strategy in ways that improve both workplace environments and the delivery of services to diverse populations.

As an Employment Relations Manager, I navigate complex employee relations matters, interpret policy, and support fair and equitable workplace practices. This role has strengthened my ability to lead with accountability, make informed decisions, and communicate effectively in high-impact situations.

In addition to my work in higher education, I bring over a decade of experience in healthcare through my role at Summit Health. Working directly with patients has provided me with a deeper understanding of how organizational decisions and public policies affect individuals in real time.

My approach to leadership is grounded in accountability, communication, and continuous improvement. I am committed to fostering environments where individuals feel supported, valued, and empowered to contribute to organizational success.

I am focused on continuing to grow as a leader while contributing to organizations in ways that strengthen equity, accountability, and meaningful service delivery.
